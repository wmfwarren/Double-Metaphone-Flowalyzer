Stats Key

Flow length is the number of words in a flow. Unique words is the number of non-repeated words. Uniqueness is the unique words divided by the flow's length. Average word length is the average number of characters in the flow's words. Word length deviation is a measure of the range of variance in length of words. Relative deviation is divination divided by the average word length.
